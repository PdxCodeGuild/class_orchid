Straw Hat Luffy

Monkey D. Luffy, also known as " Straw Hat Luffy", is the main protagonist of the manga and anime, One Piece. He is the captain of the infamous and powerful Straw Hat Pirates. His lifelong dream is to become the Pirate King by finding the legendary treasure the One Piece.
