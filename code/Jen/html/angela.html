Angela Meade

Angela Meade grew up in Centralia, Washington where she excelled at pretty much everything she ever touched. Angela was active in extra curricular activities including the Swim Team, Cheerleading Squad, Band and Choir. After graduating from Centralia High School she initially majored in medicine while using her elective time to continue her study of music. Eventually her love of music and vocal skill led her to debut at the Metropolitan Opera House. She has since performed all over the world and recently found herself near home in Seattle.
