The Rex

Grand Champion, Green Herb's Patience

White doe. This doe has some small wins. Her dense, plush coat and excellent type make her a solid contender at the 2022 ARBA convention

Grand Champion, Green Herb's Paradise City

Sable doe. This doe has yet to have a big win but lots of variety wins in large classes. She has excellent type and fur and is entered in the 2022 ARBA convention.

Grand Champion, Green Herb's November Reign

White buck. November Reign was named for his potential at the 2022 ARBA convention but although he is a gorgeous boy with plush fur and the type to match, he's also a messy boy. November Reign has two best of breeds and two best opposites.

Grand Champion, Green Herb's Rag Doll

Sable buck. A late bloomer with one BOS win and 4th in his class at the 2021 ARBA convention. He has great type, finally has beautiful fur and he has an incredibly sweet disposition. He'll be taking a more active breeding role going into 2023.

Grand Champion, Davis Malcom

White buck. Two time Best of Breed winner, four time best opposite. This buck has show wins in 2019, 2021 and 2022. He earned Grand Champion at over 2 years old.

Green Herb's Shine Bright

Sable Marten doe. Not a showable variety but an integral part of the quality of Rex at Green Herb.
