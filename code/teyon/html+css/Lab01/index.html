    Pennsylvania State University, commonly known as Penn State is a public University located in University Park, Pennsylvania. Founded in 1855, Penn State is currently home too 47,789 students and over 17,000 faculty and staff members. It is known to most for it's football team which has won 2 National Championships Chants of "We are Penn State can be heard across the campus on any given football gameday.The university began in 1955 as 'The Farmers High School' with instructions centered around agriculture beginning in 1859. In 1862 the school was renames 'The Agricultural College of Pennsylvania' and renamed again in 1953 to what is it referred too today 'The Pennsylvania State University'.
